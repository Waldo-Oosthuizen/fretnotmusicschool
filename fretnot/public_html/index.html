FAQ's

Do you need your own instrument?
It is advisable to have your own instrument so that you can practice at home. If you do not have your own you can borrow one of ours during the lesson.
Are exam courses available?
Yes, we follow the trinity syllabus if you are interested in entering for an exam.
Is there an age limit?
From 6 years and upwards.
How long are the lessons?
Lessons are 30 minutes long and done once a week.
